About me

Currently, I'm a computer science student at KTH. My professional interests are mainly algorithms and back-end systems.

I do a fair bit of competitive programming together with my team Omogen Heap, which won the Nordic programming championships four consecutive years. Lately I've mostly been organizing contests, in particular the Swedish Olympiad in Informatics.

I also organize training camps and other competitions in maths, programming, computer security etc., mostly within the organization Young Science Sports.
